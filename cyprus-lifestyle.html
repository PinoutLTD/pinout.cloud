The joy & health of your family

Among the many smart home automation solutions, the engineers at Pinout have chosen two that are especially relevant for Cyprus. This is a place where families value the gentle Mediterranean climate and strive for longevity and a healthier lifestyle — quite different from our friends in bustling megacities.

The hot climate and relaxed atmosphere of this island, beloved by many Europeans, set a special pace of life, making us all a little less anxious, a bit more carefree...

Taking care of your family's health & living a carefree life on a sunny island

Building on these features, we’ve created our first support program for private clients. After reviewing our past developments, exploring dozens of smart home solutions, and testing them in the real-life conditions of Cyprus, we developed an automation system that seamlessly fits your lifestyle.

It supports your interest in biohacking, ensures ideal conditions for healthy sleep, and helps reduce stress — giving you the ability to check and turn off the air conditioner right from a taxi on your way to a concert of your favorite band.

Don't become robots — let the robots work for you.
