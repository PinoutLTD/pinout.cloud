Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

  1. Introduction

    Welcome to Pinout.Cloud ("we," "our," or "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website [pinout.cloud].

  2. Information We Collect

    We collect the following types of information:

    • Personal Data: Name, email, phone number, etc., when voluntarily provided by you.
    • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on the site, and interactions
    • Tracking Data: We use cookies, Google Analytics, and UTM parameters to track visitor behavior and improve our services.

  3. How We Use Your Information

    We use the collected data to:

    • Improve website functionality and user experience.
    • Analyze traffic and marketing effectiveness.
    • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
    • Comply with legal obligations under European regulations.

  4. Tracking Technologies

    We use cookies, localStorage and tracking technologies to analyze user behavior. You can manage or disable it below. We also comply with GDPR by obtaining user consent before tracking.

    Update Consent:

  5. Google Analytics & UTM Tracking

    We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized data about website visits. This includes UTM parameters to track campaign performance. IP anonymization is enabled to comply with GDPR regulations.

  6. Your Rights (GDPR & European Compliance)

    Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other European privacy laws, you have the following rights:

    • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold.
    • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate data.
    • Deletion: Request data erasure ("right to be forgotten").
    • Objection: Opt-out of tracking and data collection.
    • Data Portability: : Request a structured, machine-readable copy of your data.
    • Restriction of Processing: Request a temporary halt to data processing under certain conditions.
    • Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with a data protection authority if your rights are violated.

    To exercise your rights, contact us at vm@pinout.cloud.

  7. Legal Basis for Processing (EU Users)

    For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process personal data based on the following legal grounds:

    • Consent: When you give explicit consent.
    • Legitimate Interest: For improving services and security.
    • Contractual Necessity: When processing is necessary for providing services.
    • Legal Obligations: When required by law.

  8. Third-Party Services

    We may use third-party services like Google Analytics. These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

  9. Data Security

    We implement security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or breaches.

  10. International Data Transfers

    If we transfer your data outside the European Economic Area (EEA), we ensure appropriate safeguards such as:

    • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission.
    • Compliance with recognized data protection frameworks.

  11. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

  12. Contact Us

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us in any preferable way
