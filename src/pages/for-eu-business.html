Management Companies and the Challenges of Daily Operations

Management companies are responsible for maintaining buildings in a safe and comfortable condition. Full automation of all processes is not yet possible, but today digitalization and IoT solutions can significantly simplify many tasks. For example, smart water and electricity meters allow you to analyze resource consumption and optimize costs. Sensors in engineering systems record deviations from the norm and automatically create requests for diagnostics or repairs, helping to quickly respond to problems.

In many countries, management companies have difficulty providing a full range of services, and those who successfully cope with this task are actively introducing digital technologies into the daily processes of their employees.

Why? What? how? - the answer to CMMS

CMMS – a digital assistant in building management

For effective management of facility maintenance, CMMS (Computerized Maintenance Management System) are used – specialized systems that allow you to plan work, assign responsible persons, keep records of completed tasks and generate reports.

Modern CMMS often overlap with ERP and CRM systems, blurring the boundaries between them. However, their key task is to manage physical objects and analyze data from IoT devices. CMMS can be either a standalone solution or an integrated part of a large CRM/ERP platform. Most of these systems are closed solutions that require significant licensing and support costs. In this context, it is worth noting Odoo, which, thanks to its open architecture, can be deployed on the company's own servers, providing autonomy and full control over data.

Is it possible to make real estate management even more transparent? Yes, if you use a digital twin of the building.

Digital Twin of a Building: Transparency Without Cloud Reliance

A digital twin is a virtual copy of a building that displays its current state in real time. It receives data from IoT devices, records the history of maintenance and repairs, providing full control and transparency of facility management.

How does Pinout's solution work?

On the
building side:

  • A local server based on Home Assistant controls IoT devices, including temperature, humidity, energy consumption, air quality, and others.
  • Connect flexibility: Wi-Fi, Zigbee, Z-Wave, LoRaWAN wireless protocols are supported, as well as Modbus, BACnet wired solutions for integration with existing engineering systems.
  • Automatic equipment monitoring: Pinout CMMS Report Service records faults, create encrypted reports, transmits them via a p2p network, and updates the digital twin of the building.

On the
management company or integrator side:

  • Pinout CMMS Property Management Agent receives reports and automatically creates requests in Odoo.
  • Odoo CRM allows you to manage requests, employee tasks and receive feedback from IoT devices in real time.
